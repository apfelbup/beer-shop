2012

Private Island was born out of the heads & hearts of 2 childhood friends who shard a passion for micro-breweries (Andrew Jasper & Matt Moran). They new-found hobby produced their first small batch of Private Island IPA in Matt’s kitchen.

2013

Private Island releases their first small batch of Mango Madness IPA. Every batch after sells out before it is finished.

2016

With the help of investment Private Island opens the flagship brewery in San Diego, CA.

2017

The first Wild Nights starts up at the Private Island Brewery which hosts huge locally talented musicians bringing in huge crowds every 1st of the month. The tradition continues til today.

2019

Private Island releases their first international beers and win “Best Stout in Show” in Ireland International Micro-brew invitational.

2020

Private Island continues to grow and distribution has become national but the core value of Private Island remains: To make a beer that’s taste will send you to your own private island.
